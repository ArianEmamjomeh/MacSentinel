Mac Sentinel

Lid Closure Alarm System

Automatically triggers an alarm when your MacBook lid is closed while the system is armed.

How to Use

  1. Ensure Amphetamine is running (required for audio with lid closed)
  2. Toggle the switch below to ARM the system
  3. Close your MacBook lid to trigger the alarm
  4. Toggle to DISARM to stop monitoring
